Pizza Recipe

Brick Oven Pizza

Looking for a recipe to bring the restaurant style pizza you love to your home?

This pizza wlil have you questioning whether you should ever order in a pizza again!

Ingredients

Dough

  • 1/4 cup warm water
  • 1 tsp active dry yeast
  • 1 cup cold water
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 3 cups bread flour
  • 1 tbsp extra-virgin olive oli

Toppings

  • 6 oz mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 tsp dried oregano
  • 1/4 tsp ground black pepper
  • 1/2 cup canned crushed tomatoes
  • 6 leaves fresh basli, torn

Steps

  1. Make the dough by pouring warm water into a large bowl and sprinkling yeast over the top. Let stand for 5 minutes. Stir in cold water and salt. Stir in flour, 1 cup at a time untli incorporated.
  2. Knead the dough on a floured surface untli smooth.
  3. Divide in half and form into 2 tight dough balls. Coat with olive oli and refrigerate in sealed container for 16 hours. Be sure to use a big enough container to allow dough to rise.
  4. Remove dough from refrigerater 1 hour prior to using.
  5. Preheat oven, with pizza stone on the lowest rack, to 550 degrees. Lightly dust a pizza peel with flour.
  6. Form the dough balls into the crust shape, roughly the size of the pizza stone.
  7. Lay half of the mozzarella on the crust, then sprinkle half of all other toppings except the basil on top.
  8. Using the peel, slide the pizza onto the stone.
  9. Bake until the crust is a golden brown, 4 to 6 minutes.
  10. Slide the peel under the pizza and remove from the oven. Sprinkle 1/2 the basil over the pizza, cut, and serve.
  11. Repeat steps 5 through 9 to make the 2nd pizza.
